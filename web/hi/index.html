OSLO

legal impacthackathon

Explorer HQ Oslo, June 5th-6th 2018

Slack

We have a special channel on Slack. Simply join #legal-hackathon channel and follow all the news with practical information concerning the hackathon. As organizers, we’re going to use this channel as a main means of communication throughout the event.


Coffee & water

On spot, there will be at least two kinds of beverages without limitations: hot coffee and cold tap water. Please, help yourselves.


Toilets

There are two toilets at the lower floor. Way to these toilets is marked with arrows and WC signs.


Doors at night hacking

Entrance door will be open on the first day until 22:00 (10:00 p.m.). On the second day, the door will be open since 8:00 a.m.


Lunch

On the first day (Tuesday), lunch will be served around 14:00 (2 p.m.) in a form of a buffet in the kitchen area on the upper floor. Enjoy it and feel free to pick and choose.
On the second day (Wednesday), we’re going to go outside to have lunch around 13:10 (1:10 p.m.) at a nearby canteen in Torggata 15.

Categories

We’d like to remind you that at the hackathon there are 5 categories, in which your projects will be assessed:
- legal document processing
- best blockchain-based solution
- crowd-based solution
- the craziest idea
- GDPR solution
