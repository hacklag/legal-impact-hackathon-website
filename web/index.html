Human Impact Hackathon
OSLO

Sign us!

Hacklag Human Impact Hackathon is a unique event for everyone who thinks that technology can make peoples' lives better. We believe that together we can make a real impact on our world.

Our main goal is to create projects that can become a spark for the better future. You and your team will have 24 hours to prepare something that works (proof of concept is fine!) and show it to others! Challenge accepted?

There's no technological restriction on what you can use to build your project, however, there are going to be some special categories like:

  • best blockchain-based solution
  • best Syncano-based solution
  • best AWS-based solution
  • the craziest idea

We want you to join us on this exciting adventure! No matter if you are an experienced developer or the beginning one. You don't even have to be a programmer to take part! We'll be more than happy to invite graphic & UX designers, tech enthusiasts, community workers & social activists.

You don't have a team? Don't worry! You'll have an opportunity to create one and make new friends.

If you have any questions feel free to contact us: maciejkorsan@hacklag.org
